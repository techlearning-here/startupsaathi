## The Problem

Indian founders currently need 6+ platforms:
- Co-founder matching (VenturLoop, Kagaar)
- Government schemes (YNOS - expensive)
- Registration (Startup India portal)
- Compliance (CAs)
- State schemes (28 different portals)
- Investor discovery (LinkedIn, random networking)

**College students** face additional challenges:
- No way to find co-founders across colleges
- Unaware of student-specific funding (SISFS, state grants)
- IIC quality varies greatly by college
- Lose support after graduation

## Our Solution

One affordable platform that combines:
- 🤝 Co-founder & team matching
- 🏛️ Government scheme navigator (200+ schemes)
- 📋 DPIIT registration guidance
- 💰 Funding discovery
- ✅ Compliance calendar
- 🎓 **Campus Connect** (college-specific features)
- 👨‍🏫 **Mentor Network** (guidance from experienced founders)
